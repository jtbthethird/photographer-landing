Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Main Street Hub?

Main Street Hub is the voice of more local businesses than any other company. Utilizing our proprietary technology, we offer a unique “do-it-for-you” marketing platform that integrates social media, customer reviews, web, and email marketing to help local businesses get more customers and keep them coming back, while saving them valuable time. Our team is on a mission to create thriving local economies and help businesses and customers discover community.

What kind of shots are you looking for?

We’re looking to shoot a small catalog of images that really tell the story of our local business customers. We want to capture the vibe, the people, the space, and any behind-the-scenes action that you think brings that story to life. You’re our eyes on the ground for our local business customers, and we appreciate your creative perspective.

How long is the photo shoot?

Our shoots are scheduled for up to 2 hours.

Do I need to edit my photos?

Nope! Our team of in-house photo editors handle all post-production and photo retouching.

What’s the scheduling process like?

We handle all of the scheduling between our customers and you, so you’ll communicate directly with our production team. We’ll find a time that works for you and the customer so that all you’ll need to do is arrive at the shoot!

What happens after I apply?

We will review your portfolio, and someone from the Main Street Hub Team will reach out with more information.
